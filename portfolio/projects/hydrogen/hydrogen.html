Hydrogen Fuel Cell-Powered Vehicle Project

Project Summary

As part of the annual Shell-Eco Marathon Competition, me and three other teammates designed and built a one-man hydrogen fuel cell prototype vehicle

With this vehicle, we set a new African Shell-Eco Marathon vehicle efficiency record which puts as in first place and qualified our team for the global finalist competition that year.

The Vehicle Drive-Train

My Team Responsibilities

As part of the team I worked on :-

  • The schematic design of the vehicle’s electronics.

  • Programming the Arduino microcontroller that served as the vehicle’s controller.

  • Installing and setting up the vehicle’s drivetrain which consisted of a single hub motor that operated at 48V and a buck converter that stepped down the H-cell voltage from 48V to 5V that was used to power the Arduino and other electronics.

    • Reflection

    It was extremely fulfilling to watch the vehicle we had spent so many long nights working on complete its very first lap around the racetrack.

    It was a challenging endeavor that gave me the opportunity to apply my theoretical engineering knowledge and learn so much more along the way.
